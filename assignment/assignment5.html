My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. The Dark Knight
    • Year: 2008
    • Main Actors: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Michael Caine
    • Description: A gritty crime drama where Batman battles the Joker, who wreaks havoc in Gotham City.
  2. Inception
    • Year: 2010
    • Main Actors: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page
    • Description: A mind-bending sci-fi thriller about a thief who infiltrates dreams to steal secrets.
  3. The Matrix
    • Year: 1999
    • Main Actors: Keanu Reeves, Laurence Fishburne, Carrie-Anne Moss
    • Description: A hacker discovers that the world is a simulated reality controlled by machines.
  4. Interstellar
    • Year: 2014
    • Main Actors: Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain
    • Description: A group of astronauts travels through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity.
  5. The Shawshank Redemption
    • Year: 1994
    • Main Actors: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman
    • Description: A man wrongly imprisoned for murder befriends another inmate and maintains hope for freedom.
