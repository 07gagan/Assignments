Understanding Special Characters in HTML

When writing HTML, you may need to display special characters like ampersands (&), angle brackets (< and >), and copyright symbols (©), among others.

For example, to display an ampersand, you would write &amp;. Similarly, for the less than symbol, use &lt; and for greater than, use &gt;.

Legal Symbols in HTML

  • Trademark symbol: ™
  • Registered symbol: ®
  • Copyright symbol: ©
